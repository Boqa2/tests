Bingemans

Bingemans offers fun for kids of all ages! Enjoy summer while staying cool at Big Splash water park. The fun continues with FunworX indoor playground, mini-golf, 10-pin bowling, on-site camping, great food, major esports events and more. In other words, Bingemans has something to keep everyone in your family entertained!

The Cambridge Butterfly...

While education is the cornerstone of this remarkable conservatory, families flock here to experience the wonder of a tropical paradise as they walk among the butterflies, birds and other small animals who live there. Thousands of free-flying butterflies await, including exotic species from Costa Rica and the Philippines. Bring the kids and turn into one yourself as you explore nature’s magic.

Homewood Suites

Homewood Suites by Hilton Cambridge-Waterloo, located by the 401, and Homewood Suites Waterloo-St. Jacobs, located by its famous Farmers Market and Village, both provide an ideal home away from home. With one-and-two bedroom suites with full kitchens and more, it’s no surprise TripAdvisor has included Homewood Suites by Hilton in its Top 25 Hotels for Families in Canada 3 years running. 

THEMUSEUM

If your kid’s idea of a museum is a musty, boring place, you’ve got to take them to THEMUSEUM in downtown Kitchener. This modern, interactive playground for the mind has hosted past exhibits that have celebrated everything from Avatar to the Rolling Stones. It’s a fun experience that will make you rethink what a museum can be!

African Lion Safari

Drive through the 9 km Game Reserve for an up-close look at countless birds and animals, many classified as endangered or threatened. See zebras, lions, rhinos, ostriches, and more–all freely roaming in spacious environments. African Lion Safari has earned a reputation for their care, management, and conservation of endangered species, ensuring their animals receive the best.

Canoeing the Grand

The aptly-named Grand River is an adventure awaiting groups large and small. Trips range from 90 minutes to 8 hours, and distances from 7 to 38 kilometres – with lots of places to stroll along the shallows or pull over for a picnic lunch. If you prefer a lazier trip along the river, tubing is also offered along a shorter stretch of the river. Kayaks, tandem kayaks and paddleboats are also available.

Conestoga River Horseback

Horseback riding along the banks of the scenic Conestogo River is an experience best shared with friends. Trail rides are offered all year round, and vary in length from 1, 2 and 4 hours. There is also an indoor riding arena, a meeting/event room with kitchenette, a large, attached deck, BBQ facilities, washrooms, parking on site, and a fire pit at the picnic area. Get ready to saddle-up for adventure!

Delta Hotels by Marriott Waterloo

Located in the heart of Uptown Waterloo, the Delta Waterloo is close to boutique shops, incredible restaurants and local attractions. After exploring the best the region has to offer, return to the Delta for a dip in the pool or relax in your stylish suite. Then it’s down to Proof Kitchen & Lounge for work...

Four Fathers Brewing Co.

Four Fathers began as the vision of four hockey dads – an artist, an entrepreneur, a corporate executive and a maverick – all of whom shared a love of handcrafted beer. Now located in the heart of Cambridge’s Hespeler Village, the microbrewery is renowned for making premium craft beers notable for their rich and complex character. Visit their tap room to sample their handcrafted beers and uniquely imaginative pub cuisine...

Willibald Farm Distillery & Brewery

Founded in 2017, Willibald is a first-generation farm distillery and brewery located outside of Cambridge in Ayr, Ontario. Striving to create progressive, high-quality products that respect tradition but aren’t bound by it, this is where rural craftsmanship meets urban sophistication. Enjoy seasonally focused food on their covered outdoor patio along with a glass of their finest.

Experience St. Jacobs

With Ontario’s most famous Farmers’ Market, St. Jacobs Village, the Market Road Antiques marketplace and more, this is truly as timeless a place as you’ll find in Ontario. Home to the largest population of Old Order Mennonites in Canada, you’re sure to see farmers traveling the local roads by horse & buggy. And don’t forget to visit the St. Jacobs Outlet Mall for some great deals!

Drayton Entertainment

One of Canada’s most successful professional theatre companies, Drayton Entertainment produces critically praised Broadway musicals, comedies and dramas in all seven of its Ontario theatres. Three of those stages – the Hamilton Family Theatre Cambridge, the St. Jacobs Country Playhouse and the St. Jacobs Schoolhouse Theatre – are right here in Waterloo Region. So come to play, then stay for a play.

The Charcuterie Bar

Created by food lovers for food lovers, the Charcuterie Bar in St. Jacobs provides a unique and immersive dining experience. See the butchers working their magic and witness the service team create beautiful boards from house-made and locally curated charcuterie. Their skilled staff is happy to suggest the perfect beverage selection to compliment your food.

West Montrose Covered Bridge (“Kissing Bridge”)

A landmark that’s now over 140 years old, the West Montrose Covered (“Kissing”) Bridge was built in 1881 by John Bear, a Mennonite barn builder. Whether you’re a lover of timeless craftsmanship or just looking for a romantic setting – with a nickname to match – the Kissing Bridge should be passed through during your visit here.

Hampton Inn & Suites Waterloo St. Jacobs

One of the region’s newest hotels, this new Hampton location is just minutes from Canada’s largest farmers’ market, the St. Jacobs Farmers’ Market, and is a short drive from the beautiful Village of St. Jacobs. It also provides all the comforts and amenities you need for a relaxing escape for two.
